Slide 1: Introduction
- Title: "Geomagnetic Storms: Understanding the Phenomenon"
- Subtitle: "May 2024 Events"
- Bullet points:
- Definition: Geomagnetic storms are disturbances in Earth's magnetic field caused by solar wind and coronal mass ejections.
- Importance: Affects satellite operations, communication systems, navigation, and Earth's climate.
- Objective: To visualize and analyze the May 2024 geomagnetic storms.

Slide 2: Causes of Geomagnetic Storms

- Title: "Solar Wind and Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs)"
- Images: NASA graphics illustrating solar wind and CMEs
- Bullet points:
- Solar wind: Stream of charged particles emitted by the Sun.
- CMEs: Huge clouds of plasma and magnetic field ejected from the Sun.
- Interaction with Earth's magnetic field.
